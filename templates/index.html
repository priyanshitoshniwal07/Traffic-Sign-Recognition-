What is Traffic Sign Recognition ?

Traffic Sign Recognition (TSR) is like a super-attentive passenger in your car that helps you see important road signs. Here's how it works:
* Imagine a small camera mounted on your windshield.
* TSR uses this camera to take pictures of the road ahead.
* It's super smart (thanks to artificial intelligence!) and can recognize different traffic signs it "sees" in the pictures.
* Stop signs, yield signs, speed limits - TSR can catch them all!
* If you ever miss a sign, TSR acts like your helpful passenger and gives you a friendly reminder.
This can help you:
* Drive safer by not missing important signs.
* Avoid getting tickets by remembering speed limits.
* Stay more focused on the road by having an extra pair of "eyes" looking out for signs.
